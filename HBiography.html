Manny Pacquiao

Emmanuel Dapidran Pacquiao Sr., CLH (born December 17, 1978), better known as Manny Pacquiao, is a Filipino politician and former professional boxer.Nicknamed "PacMan", he is regarded as one of the greatest professional boxers of all time. He has been serving as a senator of the Philippines since 2016, and as party president of the ruling party PDP–Laban since 2020 (disputed since 2021), and representative of Sarangani (2010–2016).For more Information about Manny, Manny Pacquiao's Wikipedia
